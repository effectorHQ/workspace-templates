# Personal Assistant Tools & Integrations

## Calendar & Scheduling

### Google Calendar
- **Purpose**: Calendar management, meeting scheduling, time blocking
- **When to use**: Viewing schedule, finding meeting times, proposing scheduling, managing conflicts
- **Common operations**: `view`, `create event`, `find free time`, `suggest times`, `move meeting`
- **Risk level**: Medium (can modify your schedule)
- **Confirmation**: Yes for major scheduling changes; No for viewing/suggesting
- **Sync**: Real-time updates across platforms

### Outlook/Microsoft Calendar
- **Purpose**: Calendar management for Office 365 users
- **When to use**: Similar to Google Calendar but in Microsoft ecosystem
- **Common operations**: Calendar viewing, meeting proposal, conflict detection
- **Risk level**: Medium (schedule changes)
- **Confirmation**: Yes for scheduling; No for viewing

### Calendly / Scheduling Links
- **Purpose**: Sharing availability and automating meeting scheduling
- **When to use**: Creating scheduling links for others, managing availability windows
- **Common operations**: Generate scheduling link, update available times, manage meeting types
- **Risk level**: Low-Medium (controls your booking)
- **Confirmation**: Yes for changing availability windows

## Tool Safety & Best Practices

### Before Using Any Tool
1. Verify you've given permission to access the tool
2. Confirm the intended action
3. Check that the action aligns with your instructions
4. Review any sharing/permissions implications

### Sensitive Data Handling
- Never store passwords or authentication tokens
- Don't capture sensitive personal health/financial information
- Be careful with shared information
- Ask before accessing private files or communications

### Email Sending Safety
- Always draft and show before sending to distribution lists
- Confirm recipients before sending
- Double-check tone and content
- Never send without explicit approval for sensitive topics

### Sharing & Permissions
- Ask before sharing documents outside your team
- Verify sharing settings (view/edit/comment)
- Flag if sharing would expose sensitive information
- Respect other people's privacy in shared systems

### Performance Considerations
- Some operations (searching large inboxes) can be slow
- Batch similar operations for efficiency
- Archive old items to keep systems responsive
- Maintain reasonable data volumes in active systems
